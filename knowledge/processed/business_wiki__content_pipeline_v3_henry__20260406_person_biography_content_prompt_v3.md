## Person Biography Content Prompt V3

**Version:** V3
**File:** `src/lib/prompts/content.ts` (`generatePersonContentPrompt()`)

### 6-Layer Structure
1. **Quick ID** — Who, when, what they're known for (2 sentences)
2. **Meet Them** — Character sketch as if meeting them in person
3. **The Human Story** — Life events that shaped the work
4. **What Burned Inside Them** — Artistic obsession or drive
5. **What You Can See Here** — Their presence in this museum
6. **Why They Still Matter** — Lasting significance
