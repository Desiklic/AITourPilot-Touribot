At AITourPilot, we believe every painting has a story. And we are using AI storytelling to bring those stories to life. Not through AR or VR, but through an AI-powered museum guide that adapts to you—your interests, your questions, your curiosity.

Imagine standing before The Ninth Wave, hearing the roar of the ocean, the legend of the ninth wave—the sailors’ greatest test. Instead of reading a plaque, you ask the painting itself:

💬 “Why is it called the Ninth Wave?”
💬 “Did Aivazovsky see a storm like this in real life?”
💬 “What happened to the survivors?”

This is what AITourPilot does. We personalize art’s stories, making every museum visit immersive, interactive, and unforgettable.
