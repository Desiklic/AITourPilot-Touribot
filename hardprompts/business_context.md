# AITourPilot — Business Context

*Always loaded into Touri's context. Condensed from product overview, campaign research, and engine docs.*

## What AITourPilot Is

A conversational AI audio guide for museums. Visitors have real-time spoken dialogue with an AI that knows the building — no screens, no QR codes. Voice-first, BYOD (visitor's own phone).

- **B2B2C model**: museums integrate the platform; visitors access via smartphone
- **Per-museum AI agents**: trained exclusively on each museum's verified content
- **30+ languages** through real-time voice AI (not pre-recorded translations)
- **Spatial intelligence**: WiFi-based room-level positioning, dwell-time tracking, flow analytics
- **No hardware burden**: eliminates physical audio guide devices entirely

## Key Differentiators

| Traditional Audio Guide | AITourPilot |
|---|---|
| Pre-recorded scripts | Real-time conversation |
| One-way narration | Two-way dialogue with Q&A |
| 8-12 pre-recorded languages | 30+ real-time |
| Dedicated devices | Visitor's own phone |
| No data insights | Dwell time, flow patterns, topic analytics |

**No competitor offers real-time spoken AI conversation.** Smartify (700+ partners), Gesso, Cuseum, izi.TRAVEL (25K+ tours) all deliver pre-recorded or text-based content.

## Pilot Structure

**Duration:** 90 days | **Scope:** 1-2 exhibition rooms

| Option | Structure | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Paid Pilot** | EUR 8,000-12,000 for 90 days (setup + 2 languages + support). Full implementation fee waived if converting within 30 days. | Museums with confirmed innovation budget |
| **Success-Share** | No upfront fee. EUR 1.50-2.00 per active session, capped at EUR 5,000. | Hesitant prospects |

**Success metrics:** Activation rate (target 15-30%), average conversation turns (5+), dwell time delta, visitor satisfaction (4.0/5.0).
