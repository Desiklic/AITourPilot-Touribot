About me


I’m an AI and digital transformation specialist and product innovator, with a background in building human-centered experiences at scale, including award-winning work at LEGO.

Today, I work on conversational AI at the intersection of storytelling, culture, and human experience. I’m the founder of AITourPilot, an AI-powered museum guide that turns silent exhibition spaces into places of discovery through conversation.

My motivation is simple: too many people walk past extraordinary artworks without ever accessing the human stories behind them. With AITourPilot, I explore how audio-first, conversational AI can create emotional connections with art—allowing visitors to ask questions, follow their curiosity, and experience culture in a more personal and accessible way.

About me

I work at the intersection of conversational AI, storytelling, and cultural experiences.
I’m the founder of AITourPilot, an AI-powered guide that transforms museum visits into personalized, dialogue-based journeys.

My focus is on building human-centered AI systems that go beyond static audio guides — enabling visitors to explore art and culture through natural conversation, curiosity, and narrative. I’m especially interested in how AI can create emotionally engaging, accessible experiences for diverse audiences, both on-site and beyond the visit itself.
