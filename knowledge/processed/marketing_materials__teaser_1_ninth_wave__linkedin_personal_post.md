🎨 What If a Painting Could Talk?

When I was a child, I stood in front of great paintings, fascinated and spellbound, my mind filling with stories that emerged from the scenes in front of me.

In the video, you see my daughter standing before The Ninth Wave by Ivan Aivazovsky. The wind lifts her hair, as if pulled from the painting itself. The sea moves. The survivors struggle. The story of the painting comes to life.

I trained an AI model (LoRA) based on her, allowing her to step into these worlds. And through AITourPilot, we are creating an AI-powered museum guide that doesn’t just tell you facts—it tells the story behind the art. The questions, the mysteries, the human struggles that shaped these masterpieces.

This is why we are building AITourPilot—not to give dry facts, but to let everyone step into the moment, feel the tension, and hear the history. To transform a museum visit into an experience where art speaks to you.
