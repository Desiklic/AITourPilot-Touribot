## Exhibition Content Prompt V3

**Version:** V3
**File:** `src/lib/prompts/content.ts` (`generateExhibitionContentPrompt()`)

### 7-Layer Structure
1. **Hook** — One sentence capturing the exhibition's draw
2. **Quick ID** — What, when, where, how many works
3. **Step Into This Room** — Spatial experience
4. **What to Notice First** — Visual priorities for guided seeing
5. **The Story This Room Tells** — Curatorial narrative
6. **Hidden Conversations** — Connections between works in the show
7. **Henry's Suggestion** — Navigation tip for the visitor's journey

See the full prompt in `src/lib/prompts/content.ts`.
