## **🎨 About AITourPilot**  
AITourPilot is an **AI-powered audio guide** that transforms museum visits by **bringing artists and paintings to life through interactive storytelling.** Unlike traditional museum audio guides, AITourPilot adapts its narration based on user preferences and engagement, making art **more personal and immersive** for visitors.

🔹 **Product Positioning:** AITourPilot is NOT an AR/VR experience but an advanced **AI-powered museum guide.**  
🔹 **Target Audience:**
   - **B2B:** Museum decision-makers (for partnerships & demo requests).
   - **B2C:** Museum visitors & art lovers (for early access signups).  
🔹 **Key Hashtag:** #FeelThePaint (Play on words: "Feel the Pain" → "Feel the Paint")  
🔹 **Core Messaging:** *"Bringing art to life. Experience the future of museum tours with AITourPilot."*
