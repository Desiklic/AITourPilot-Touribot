AITourPilot

Onboarding

Introduction

We have created a technological solution designed to enhance the user experience in museums. We trust it completely. For this reason, during this trial phase, we offer three museums the opportunity to implement AITourPilot without any initial cost.

This way, we aim to build a mutually beneficial alliance that improves visitor engagement and learning through personalized, AI-generated interactive tours while fine-tuning our technology.

2. Onboarding Process

Timeline Overview
	1.	Learning and Integration
	2.	User Testing
	3.	Implementation
	4.	End of Free Phase
	5.	Maintenance and Analysis

Duration: 19 weeks, iterative process.

Phase 1: Learning and Integration

Objective
Integrate AITourPilot with the museum’s specific content, enriching the visitor experience with deep and contextual knowledge.

Steps
	1.	Data Collection: The museum provides a comprehensive list of artworks, bibliography, and other relevant resources.
	2.	Integration: Develop a system that connects works beyond traditional parameters, enabling unique discoveries.

Duration: Week 1

Phase 2: User Testing

Objective
Refine the accuracy and suitability of AITourPilot through initial testing phases.

Steps
	1.	Participation: Select users, museum staff, and some visitors to take part in a pilot test.
	2.	Co-Creation: Offer the museum an active role in improving and customizing the AI Tour Pilot content.

Duration: Week 3

Phase 3: Implementation

Objective
Official launch of AITourPilot for museum visitors.

Details:
	•	Initial Free Period: Following the learning and testing phases, AITourPilot is offered free of charge for three months.
	•	Cost Structure Afterward:
	•	Revenue-sharing model: AITourPilot takes 50% of revenue generated from the app.
	•	Fixed fee: €2.50 per user if the museum decides not to charge visitors for the app.
	•	Combined ticket and app pricing (e.g., €20 for entry + app): App is priced at €5, and the museum pays €2.50 per user.

Duration: Week 7–19

Phase 4: Maintenance and Analysis

Objective
Ensure the ongoing optimization and updates of AITourPilot to adapt to the museum’s evolving needs.

Services Offered:
	•	Custom Updates: Flexibility in content updates, design, and data analysis.
	•	Annual Updates: Free annual updates of artworks in the app to keep the content fresh and engaging.

3. Opportunities with AITourPilot

Visitor Diversification: Promote greater appreciation of the museum’s collection. (History)
User Experience (UX): Adapt tours based on individual preferences, increasing satisfaction and engagement. (UX - User Experience)
Accessibility: Lower entry barriers for a broader audience, making the museum experience welcoming and accessible regardless of visitors’ artistic knowledge. (Customers)
Commercialization: Leverage AITourPilot data for targeted marketing strategies and personalized store recommendations. (Sales)

Break the “Mona Lisa Effect”
Encourage discovery beyond the most famous works.

4. Commitment to Flexibility

We believe in art as a transformative tool for society. For this reason, we aim to make museums accessible to everyone.

We understand that each institution has its own particularities. At AITourPilot, we are open to discussions to reach agreements that facilitate collaboration.
