# Pipeline Stage Guide

*Reference for stage transitions and pipeline management.*

## Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Name | Entry Criteria | Next Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Identified | Added to database, basic info | Research the museum and contact |
| 1 | Researched | Website, LinkedIn, news reviewed | Build personalization package |
| 2 | Personalized | Insight package + hook line ready | Draft and send first email |
| 3 | Outreach Sent | First email sent | Wait 3-5 days, then Message 2 |
| 4 | In Sequence | Messages 2 or 3 sent | Monitor for reply |
| 5 | Responded | Reply received, scored | Act on score (see objection handling) |
| 6 | Demo Scheduled | Meeting booked | Prepare personalized demo |
| 7 | Demo Completed | Demo done | Send follow-up + pilot proposal |
| 8 | Proposal Sent | Pilot proposal delivered | Follow up in 1 week |
| 9 | Negotiating | Active deal discussion | Support with answers, case studies |
| 10 | Closed | Won / Lost / Long-term Nurture | Celebrate or set nurture cadence |

## Stale Contact Rules

- **Stage 3-4 (In sequence):** If no reply after 10 days post-Message 3, move to nurture (Stage 10, Long-term)
- **Stage 5 (Responded, score 3):** Follow up at the interval they suggested, or 30 days default
- **Stage 6-7 (Demo):** Follow up within 48 hours of demo
- **Stage 8-9 (Proposal/Negotiating):** Weekly check-in, never let >7 days pass without contact

## Museum Tier Classification

| Tier | Profile | Examples | Approach |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1 — Heritage** | UNESCO-listed, 500K-5M visitors, rich archives, complex narratives | La Pedrera, SS Great Britain, MAH Geneva | Enterprise deal, full spatial intelligence |
| **Tier 2 — Progressive** | Innovation-forward, 100K-500K visitors, digital strategy exists | Cap Sciences, Slot Loevestein | Pilot-first, prove ROI |
| **Tier 3 — Opportunistic** | Smaller, niche, opportunistic fit | Local museums, specialty collections | Success-share, minimal scope |
