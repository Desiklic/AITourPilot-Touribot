Thank you for your thoughtful critique, Marietta — I truly appreciate your passion for protecting the mystery and emotional depth that make Vermeer's work timeless.

I understand the concern: by interpreting art through AI, we might flatten its ambiguity or close down the place for personal imagination. But AITourPilot was created with the opposite goal in mind.

Many visitors today feel overwhelmed or disconnected in front of a masterpiece. They don’t always have the context or confidence to engage deeply. Our goal is not to present “the one AI truth” but to spark questions, surface different layers of meaning, and create a more personal, emotional starting point for exploration.

We want to revive curiosity, not resolve it.

What excites forward-thinking museums is that AITourPilot isn't a definitive narrator, but a conversational companion — helping visitors feel connected enough to linger, wonder, and form their own perspectives.

And I'd argue that reviving that kind of emotional engagement — especially for those who might otherwise look away — is deeply in line with Vermeer's spirit.

Thanks again for raising this valuable conversation.

– Hermann Kudlich, Co-Founder of AITourPilot
