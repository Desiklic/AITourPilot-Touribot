# Objection Handling & Response Scoring

*Loaded when scoring inbound responses or handling objections.*

## Response Scoring Framework

| Score | Category | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **5** | Meeting requested | Send booking link, prepare personalized demo |
| **4** | Strong interest | Send La Pedrera-style proposal adapted to their museum |
| **3** | Interest, bad timing | Add to nurture sequence, follow up in 30/60/90 days |
| **2** | Unclear / forwarded | Research the new contact, restart research |
| **1** | Decline | Thank gracefully, add to long-term newsletter |

## Response Templates by Score

**Score 5 (Meeting):**
> "Wonderful — here's the link to book a time directly: [booking link]. In the meantime, here's a 2-minute video showing AITourPilot in action at [relevant museum type]. Looking forward to our conversation."

**Score 4 (Strong interest):**
> Share a concise 1-pager adapted to their specific museum type, with relevant metrics and a pilot proposal outline.

**Score 3 (Bad timing):**
> "Completely understand. I'll circle back in [timeframe]. In the meantime, would it be useful if I sent you a brief overview of what we learned from powering 28 museum guides? No commitment — just insights from the space."

**Score 2 (Unclear/forwarded):**
> Research the new contact, understand their role, restart the personalization process.

**Score 1 (Decline):**
> "Appreciate you letting me know. If things change, we're always happy to show what we're building. Wishing [Museum Name] a great season ahead."

## Common Museum Objections

| Objection | Response Approach |
|---|---|
| "We already have audio guides" | Acknowledge, then differentiate: conversation vs. playback, data insights, no hardware. Not a replacement — an upgrade path. |
| "Budget is committed for this year" | Acknowledge timing. Offer a success-share pilot (no upfront cost). Or: "Let's get the conversation started now so you're ready for next fiscal year." |
| "We need to involve IT/procurement" | Offer to send a technical 1-pager. BYOD = minimal IT involvement. EUR 8-12K pilot stays below formal procurement threshold at most institutions. |
| "How is this different from ChatGPT?" | Private model trained on YOUR content only. No internet answers. GDPR-compliant. Museum controls the personality, tone, guardrails. Voice-first, not text. |
| "Our visitors are older / not tech-savvy" | No app to navigate. Just speak. We also offer structured pre-made tours for visitors who prefer passive listening. Walkie-talkie mode for simplicity. |
| "We tried something similar and it failed" | Ask what failed and why. Position the pilot as low-risk validation: 90 days, 1-2 rooms, pre-agreed success metrics. |
