# AITourPilot LinkedIn Campaign Details & Setup Guide

## Spring 2025 Campaign — Reconstruction Blueprint

---

## 1. Campaign Architecture

### 3-Phase Approach (Proven Model)

| Phase | Objective | Tactic | Budget | Duration |
|-------|-----------|--------|--------|----------|
| **Phase 1: Teasers** | Brand Awareness & Reach | Boost video teasers (Sponsored Video) | EUR 100 per teaser | 4 days each |
| **Phase 2: Retargeting** | Re-engagement | Retarget engagers from Phase 1 | ~$10/day for 7 days ($70) | 7 days |
| **Phase 3: Conversion** | Sign-ups & Partnerships | Lead Gen / Website Clicks | EUR 100 | 4 days |

**Total budget deployed in Spring 2025:** ~EUR 350 across paid LinkedIn ads (excluding organic posting effort).

### Campaign Settings That Worked

- **Objective:** Brand Awareness
- **Optimization Goal:** Reach
- **Bidding Strategy:** Maximum Delivery (spend full budget)
- **Daily Budget:** EUR 25.00
- **Duration per teaser:** 4 days (Mon-Thu or Tue-Fri)
- **Ad Format:** Sponsored Video (not single image, not carousel)
- **Placement:** LinkedIn only (no Audience Network)

## 2. Audience Segmentation

### English Campaign (Primary — 150,000+ members)

**Geographic targeting (Recent or Permanent):**
- **US Cities:** New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Houston, Philadelphia, San Francisco Bay Area, Washington DC-Baltimore Area, Boston
- **Europe:** United Kingdom, Netherlands, Finland, Norway, Denmark, Sweden, DACH (Germany/Austria/Switzerland)

**Profile Language:** English

**Job Title Targeting (Current) — ANY of the following:**
- Art Curator, Museum Curator, Assistant Curator, Museum Director
- Art Historian, Museum Educator, Museum Collections Manager, Head of Collections
- Arts Programmer, Exhibit Manager, Interactive Art Director, Digital Art Director
- Innovation Lead, Innovation Consultant, Chief Innovation Officer
- Creative Project Manager, Digital Strategy Director
- Technology Innovation Manager, Digital Transformation Manager/Advisor/Executive/Officer
- Vice President of Digital Transformation, Director Technology Innovation
- Senior Director of Marketing, Marketing Innovation Manager, Director Marketing Innovation
- Director Marketing Communications

**OR Company Industries:** Museums

**Exclusion:** Bristol City Council (specific exclusion)

**Audience Expansion:** OFF for Teaser 1 EN, ON for Teaser 2 EN

### German Campaign (Secondary — 250,000+ members)

**Geographic targeting:** DACH only

**Profile Language:** German

**Targeting Logic (AND structure — more restrictive):**
- **Company Industries:** Marketing Services, Museums, Travel Arrangements, Hospitality, Museums/Historical Sites/Zoos, Performing Arts, Higher Education, Advertising Services, Libraries
- **AND Job Functions:** Business Development, Arts and Design, Marketing, Program and Project Management, Product Management

**Exclusions:** Entry, Training, and Unpaid seniority levels

**Audience Expansion:** ON

### Lead Generation Campaign (Phase 3 — Vermeer)

Shifted from Brand Awareness to Lead Generation objective. Used LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms directly (no landing page friction).

## 4. Dual-Account Posting Strategy

Every teaser was posted from **two LinkedIn accounts simultaneously**, each with a distinct voice:

| Account | Tone | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Hermann Kudlich (Personal)** | Founder storytelling, emotional, behind-the-scenes, personal journey | Leverage existing network, build trust through personal narrative |
| **AITourPilot (Company Page)** | Professional, museum-innovation focused, thought leadership | Build company brand, attract B2B leads |

This dual approach was critical given the company page started with only 3-5 followers.

## 6. Key Recommendations for Next Campaign

1. **Keep the EUR 25/day, 4-day format** — it proved cost-effective across all teasers
2. **English campaign outperformed German** — consider allocating more budget to EN
3. **Enable Audience Expansion selectively** — it significantly increased reach for Teaser 2
4. **Lead Gen forms** reduced friction vs. landing page clicks — use for conversion phase
5. **The AND-targeting for German** (industry AND function) was too restrictive — consider OR-logic
6. **Add Nordics more prominently** — Copenhagen and Netherlands showed strong organic resonance
7. **Expand beyond museums to adjacent industries:** Higher Ed, Libraries, Performing Arts, Tourism
8. **Use the "paintings come alive" video format** — this was the creative breakthrough that drove engagement
9. **Cross-platform amplification** — use LinkedIn organic posts + YouTube Shorts + TikTok simultaneously
10. **Personal account posting is essential** — founder storytelling consistently outperformed company page posts
