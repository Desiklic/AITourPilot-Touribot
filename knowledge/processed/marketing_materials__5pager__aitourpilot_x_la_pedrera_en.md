THE CHALLENGE
La Pedrera's Challenge
The knowledge gap. La Pedrera possesses vast documentation—architectural archives, scientific
research, historical analysis, connections to buildings and archives across Barcelona. Decades of
accumulated expertise. Today, visitors access only a limited fraction of it. Current audio guides deliver
the same pre-established script to every visitor. An architecture student and a family with children get
the same experience. The knowledge exists. The activation does not.
The operational gap. 3,300 visitors per day—with peak days exceeding 4,500—and no real-time
visibility into how they move through the building. Where congestion builds, how long people stay,
whether the visit stays within its intended duration—today this depends on staff observation, not data.
The hardware burden. Hundreds of physical devices to charge, distribute, collect, clean, maintain, and
replace. Every day. La Pedrera is explicitly not looking for another hardware-dependent solution.
The ambition. What La Pedrera is seeking goes far beyond a better audio guide:
Personalised content that adapts to each visitor's interests and language
Interactive engagement where visitors ask questions and receive real-time answers
Visitor behaviour data—movement, dwell times, topics, preferences
Pre-visit planning and post-visit follow-up
Technology that supports human guides, not replaces them
This is the ISE 2026 Open Innovation Challenge. It maps precisely to what AITourPilot was built to
deliver.

YOUR QUESTIONS—OUR ANSWERS
From Our Meeting on February 12
During our conversation, several important questions were raised. Here are our direct responses.
"How do you support visitors who don't want to interact with AI—like elderly visitors who prefer a
fixed tour?"
AITourPilot supports pre-made, structured tours alongside the conversational mode. Curated routes with
prepared content that plays at each location—similar to a traditional audio guide but drawing from the full
depth of La Pedrera's archives. The system knows where the visitor is and delivers the right content
automatically. Both modes run on the same platform. The visitor chooses at the start.
"What about reliability? You're running 24/7 with hundreds of visitors every day."
Non-negotiable. We work with industry-leading partners, chosen for reliability and quality—not cost. SLA-
backed support with guaranteed response times. Graceful degradation ensures visitors never see an error:
cached content continues, static backup activates. Enterprise-grade cloud architecture with no single point
of failure. 24/7 system monitoring with proactive alerting. We built this for institutions that cannot afford
downtime.
"What about group tours—a group listening to the same AI together?"
An excellent co-development opportunity. Group mode: one AI guide serves the whole group; any member
asks, everyone hears the answer. Seamless switch: individual visitors can detach and continue their own
conversation at any point. Technology that strengthens the guide's role, not competes with it.
"Can we upload our own content? Do we have a CMS?"
Yes. Your team uploads, organises, and updates the knowledge base directly. Equally important: we develop
the AI behaviour layer together—personality, tone, guardrails, content priorities. Your institutional expertise
shapes the AI. Our technical expertise makes it work. The result: an AI guide that represents La Pedrera
authentically, because it was built with La Pedrera.
