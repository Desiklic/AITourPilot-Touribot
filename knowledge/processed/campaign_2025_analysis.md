## Reactivation Campaign: The 74 Warm Contacts

### Why These Are Not Cold

They signed up because the AI art videos emotionally resonated. Interest decayed, but the emotional anchor remains. 11 months old, but:

- **Email decay is minimal** (addresses still valid)
- **Emotional decay is recoverable** (with the right angle)

### Reactivation Strategy (Before New Outreach)

**Do NOT send:** "Hey, are you still interested?"

**DO send:** "You were one of the first people who believed in this."

### Sequence for Museum Professionals (HubSpot list -- 31 leads)

**Email 1: "It's real now"**

> Subject: From teaser to reality -- AITourPilot update
>
> Hi [First Name],
>
> Almost a year ago, you signed up after seeing our Vermeer teaser. That video showed a vision. Today I'm writing because the vision is real.
>
> Since then: 28 museums across 5 languages. Award-winning ("Best Emerging Cultural AI Experience 2026"). A content factory that transforms museum archives into live AI voice guides in under 24 hours.
>
> I'd love to show you what it's become. Book a 30-minute demo directly:
>
> [Google Calendar booking link]
>
> Best,
> Hermann

**Email 2 (Day 5): Specific value for their museum**

Use Module 3 to personalize. Reference their institution. Include one insight.

**Email 3 (Day 10): Social proof + light urgency**

> We're onboarding our first pilot partners this spring. If [Museum Name] is exploring visitor engagement innovation, this might be interesting timing.

### Sequence for General Subscribers (MailerLite -- 43 contacts)

Different angle: these are a mix of art enthusiasts and museum-adjacent professionals.

**Email 1: "The app is ready -- be among the first"**

> You signed up for AITourPilot almost a year ago. Today, the app is live with 28 museums in 5 languages. You can try it right now.
>
> [App link / web demo link]
