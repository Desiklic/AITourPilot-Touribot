## Museum Overview Content Prompt V3

**Version:** V3
**File:** `src/lib/prompts/content.ts` (`generateMuseumContentPrompt()`)

### 7-Layer Structure
1. **Hook** — One sentence that captures the museum's essence
2. **Quick ID** — Pure orientation: what it is, where, what it holds
3. **The Building Speaks** — Spatial experience of arriving and entering
4. **A Story Worth Telling** — Founding story or key transformation
5. **What You'll Discover** — Gallery highlights as a journey
6. **Why This Place Matters** — Emotional significance
7. **Connection** — How this museum connects to the city and culture

See the full prompt in `src/lib/prompts/content.ts`.
