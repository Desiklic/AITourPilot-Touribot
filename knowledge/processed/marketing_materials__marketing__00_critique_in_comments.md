Thanks for sharing these critiques — they’re rich, thoughtful, and show exactly where sensitivity, nuance, and clarity are needed in your messaging.

Here's what I took from the feedback and your direction:

---

### 🔍 **Insights from the Critiques:**

- **“Passive visitors”** can sound dismissive — both of museums *and* their audiences  
- There’s fear that AI **replaces** rather than **supports** human perception and interpretation  
- Concern that AI may flatten nuance, imagination, and individual perspective  
- Worry about **tech-driven disconnection** and **environmental impact**  
- A shared belief that **the artwork must remain central** — the AI should enhance, not overshadow  

---

### 🎯 **Reframed Ad Goals:**

- Speak from the heart of your mission: **sparking curiosity**, not replacing it  
- Clearly frame AI as a **complementary, human-centered tool**  
- Emphasize conversation over automation, and depth over convenience  
- Position AITourPilot as a **means of emotional connection**, not distraction

---
